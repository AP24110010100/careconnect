Wellness Resources

Simple guidelines to support your long-term health.

Sleep Routine

Aim for 7-8 hours of sleep per night. Keep your schedule consistent and avoid bright screens 1 hour before resting.

Hydration Focus

Drink adequate water throughout the day. Minor dehydration can severely inhibit focus and cause resting brain fog.

Physical Activity

Just 30 minutes of light daily exercise naturally boosts endorphins, physically mitigating stress loops.

Breathing Strategy

When acute stress hits, use Box Breathing: Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 4, exhale for 4, and hold empty for 4.
